Grandma's Perfect Apple Pie

Serving:8 slices Prep-Time: 30 minutes Baking-Time: 45-50 minutes

What You Need

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 6 to 7 cups thinly sliced peeled tart apples
  • Dough for double-crust pie
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 large egg white

How To Cook

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.In a small bowl, combine sugars, flours and spices; set aside. In a large bowl, toss apples with lemon juice. Add sugar mixture; toss to coat
  2. On a lightly floured surface, rolls one half of dough to a 1/8-in thick circle; transfer to a 9-inch pie plate.Trim even with a rim. Add filling; dot with butter. Roll remiang dogh to a 1/8 inch thick circle. Place over filling. Trim, seal and flute edge. Cut slits on top. Beat egg white until foamy; brush over crust. Sprinkle it with sugar. Cover the edge loosely with foil.
  3. Bake for 25 minutes.Remove foil; bake until crust is golden brown and filling is bubbly, 20-25 minutes longer. Cook on a wire rack.
